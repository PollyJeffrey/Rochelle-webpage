General information

This year I am coaching at Crosland Heath Golf Club in Huddersfield. My coaching sessions take place in the practice area of the club, where we have access to the driving range, one of the best short game areas in Huddersfield, and the undercover studio. I have three years of tour playing experience and a level 3 golf qualification, plus years of acquired knowledge to pass on to my pupils. I coach anyone from complete beginners to professionals. Coaching is tailored to your needs, whether you need help technically or are wanting to improve your scores through performance coaching, I have the knowledge to help you improve your game in every area.

Junior coaching

Through the winter I run blocks of junior group coaching sessions on a Sunday morning, coinciding with the school half-terms. I have a younger group (ages 5-8) at 10am followed by an older group (ages 9-14) at 11am. The children use real golf clubs (which can be provided) and we do different activities and games each week to introduce them to every aspect of the game. The cost per child for a block of six weeks is £40. I also run junior tournaments for the children, priced at £10 a tournament. They give the children a real feel for the game and spark their competitive edge. Contact me on 07540085820 or email me at roch_morris@hotmail.co.uk if you are interested in group coaching for your child.

Coaching prices and packages

Areas of coaching that can be covered are, full swing, short game, putting, performance coaching and on-course coaching. I charge £30 for a single one hour lesson, or £100 for a block of four one hour lessons. These lessons will be on the range and practice area, with on-course lessons charged at an hourly rate. I have times available on Sundays 9am/12pm/1pm/2pm and Fridays 9am/10am/11am/12pm. Individual lessons can be booked for adults and for juniors at a discounted rate of £25 per hour. Contact me on 07540085820 or email me at roch_morris@hotmail.co.uk to book a lesson and improve your golf game!
