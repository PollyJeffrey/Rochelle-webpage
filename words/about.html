Early Career

I joined Crosland Heath golf club when I was 8 years old. I started playing competitively at the age of 10 years old, and from there I went on to represent Yorkshire juniors 2nd team, then on to the 1st team, eventually being named an honouree member of Yorkshire. In 2016 I was announced as a member of the GB&I Curtis Cup team following in the shoes of Michelle Wie, Laura Davies, Lexi Thompson and Charley Hull to name a few.
