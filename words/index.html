Meet the team

I have a great team behind me, supporting me every step of the way. To find out more about each of them please take a look at their websites or social media profiles.

John Eyre

Swing coach

John has been my coach since I was 8 years old whilst at Crosland Heath Golf Club and has been instrumental in developing my game. He's a qualified AA PGA Professional and is based at Woodsome Hall in Huddersfield.

Matt Murray

Personal trainer

Matt has been my strength and conditioning coach since 2021. He is a golf specific personal trainer and his experience is helping me to improve my fitness and mobility in relation to the golf swing. He is based at Metcon Gyms in Whitworth.

Craig Coulson

Physiotherapist

I have been working with Craig since 2016, improving my mobility to aid swing improvements and dealing with any injuries that arise. He is based at Elmwood Physio in Holmfirth.

Roger Morris

Caddy

My dad, Roger, is a regular caddy for me on tour! Playing off a four handicap himself, he inspired me to start playing golf when I was 5 years old. His years of experience playing are a real asset on the course.
